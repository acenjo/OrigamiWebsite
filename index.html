Here is an origami fold of a camel, and some facts about them!

Interesting facts about camels:

  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.

Here is an origami fold of a chameleon, and some facts about them!

Interesting facts about chameleons:

  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.
  • Their hands and feet have large toes that help them cling to branches.
  • Chameleons are solitary animals. Forced handling or unwanted handling can cause hissing and biting.

Here is an origami fold of a pigeon, and some facts about them!

Interesting facts about pigeons:

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  • Pigeons are highly sociable animals.

Here is an origami fold of a teddy bear, and some facts about them!

Interesting facts about teddy bears:

  • Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902.
  • The first toy stuffed bear was created by German toymaker Margarete Steiff.

Here is an origami fold of a panda, and some facts about them!

Interesting facts about pandas:

  • They have great camouflage for their environment.
  • Their eyes are different to normal bears.
  • Bamboo is critical to their diet.

Here is an origami fold of a flying cicada, and some facts about them!

Interesting facts about cicadas:

  • Cicadas are mostly beneficial. They prune mature trees, aerate the soil, and once they die, their bodies serve as an important source of nitrogen for growing trees.
  • Cicadas are among the loudest insects known to man, and a swarm of them can produce sounds up to 120 decibels.
